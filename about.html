About us

We make gluten-free vegan cupcakes in the heart of Lisbon, you can come by and take them with you or order them at your convenience and pick them up in our store

All of our cakes are 100% vegan and gluten-free, we choose organic and locally sourced ingredients whenever possible, so you can enjoy the tastiest and healthiest cupcakes you have ever tasted!
